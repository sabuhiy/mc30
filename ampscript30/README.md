# AMPscript30 - AMPscript for Dynamic Content

Starter resources for the [AMPscript30 30-day challenge](https://marketingcloud30.com/challenges/), where you practice AMPscript to personalize and automate email content in Marketing Cloud.

## What's here

| Folder | Contents |
|--------|----------|
| `datasets/` | Sample CSV data to import as data extensions for the challenges. |
| `email_templates/` | Starter email templates to build your AMPscript into. |

This repo holds the **starter files only** - the scenarios and solutions live on the website.

## How to use

1. Sign up for AMPscript30 at [marketingcloud30.com](https://marketingcloud30.com/challenges/).
2. Import the dataset(s) a day references and open the matching template.
3. Add your AMPscript, then check your result against the solution on the challenge page.
